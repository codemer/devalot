h2. Q: How do I create an account? A: If the site administrator has enabled new account creation you can go to the "login page":/account/login and click "Register For a New Account". h2. Q: What does it mean that my account is moderated? A: In order to keep spam to a minimum, new accounts start out with zero points, meaning they are moderated. As soon as a moderator reviews your content, you will be promoted to a Level 2 user and your content will no longer be moderated. See [[Moderation Levels]] for a list of levels. h2. Q: This is a pretty cool application, can I install my own copy? A: You sure can! Head over to the main project page.
